About Carpantry Creations


We are a business that managed to combine the physical labor of wood working with the technical skills required to build a online business. We did this through hard work and great cooperation. We believe that everyone should have access to basic necessities such as bed frames and tables so we decided that we could build them ourselves and sell them at low prices for the masses! We also allow people to get these items in any types of wood they so desire in order to be very customer friendly.
